Belly Button Biodiversity Dashboard

Use the interactive charts below to explore the dataset

The information presented on this webpage aims to show data that has been collected for several bacteria species that were found to be present within the bellybuttons of certain individuals. This is in order to find the best species of these bacteria with which to use in the production of synthetic beef. The collected data is presented in the form of a horizontal bar chart, a bubble chart and finally a gauge chart specifically to observe the frequency of bellybutton washing done by the candidates. Feel free to make use of the drop-down menu below to observe the trends for each of the candidates.
